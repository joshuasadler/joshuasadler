About Me

I’m a freelance video editor, writer, and content creator with a passion for storytelling. Storytelling is at the heart of everything I do — whether I’m editing, writing scripts, or directing, I believe great content starts with a great story. Whether you're a cooking channel, an adventure creator, or a language-learning platform, I’m here to help you tell your story in the most engaging and authentic way possible.
